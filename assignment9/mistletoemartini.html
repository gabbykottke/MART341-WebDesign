Mistletoe Martini

Ingredients

  • 1.5 oz Vodka
  • 1.5 oz Cranberry Juice
  • 1/2 oz St. Germain Elderflower Liqeur
  • 1/2 oz. Simply Syrup
  • Mint and Fresh Cranberries for Garnish

Instructions

1. In a shaker, combine vodka, cranberry juice, St. Germain elderflower liqeur, and simple syrup with ice.
2. Shake well and strain over ice into a chilled glass.
3. Garnish with fresh cranberries and mint.
4. Enjoy your Mistletoe Martini!
