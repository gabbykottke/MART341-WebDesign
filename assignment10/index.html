Sims 4 Expansion Packs

These are two of my favorite expansion packs for Sims 4

City Living Expansion Pack

This expansion pack brought apartments to the game as well as more activities for your sim to go to. I love the festivals and flea markets, as they added a lot more variety to the game play itself. It made it a lot more fun to start off your sim's life as you didn't have to spend all of your money on a house immediately. It also brought a lot of different cultures into the game making it a much more vibrant world.

Cottage Living Expansion Pack

I love cottage living for the aesthetic it brought to the game. It brought farming into the game along with a world with a very distinct look which paired with the new furniture and clothing items. This expansion pack is perfect for a calm playthrough, raising chickens and llamas and helping your neighbors run errands makes the game feel very homey. I also love the lot challenges such as simple living, which makes it necessary to have food items in your inventory to actually make food.
