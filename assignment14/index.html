Welcome to Boy in the Boat Pottery!

Welcome to Boy in the Boat Pottery, which creates handcrafted pottery locally in Missoula, Montana. Every piece is made with love and care. Whether you are searching for decor, jewelry, or functional everyday pieces you have reached the right place. This website showcases the artworks of James Dea, all of which are made at the Missoula Clay Studio. He is a studio resident working in both handbuilding and wheel thrown artworks.
