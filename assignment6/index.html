Salty Nut Tart with Rosemary


Ingredients

  • 1 cup pine nuts
  • 1 cup walnuts
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons light corn syrup
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon fresh rosemary
  • Sweet Tart Dough
  • 1/4 teaspoon flaky salt for serving

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 and toast the pine nuts and walnuts for 6-9 minutes until they are golden brown and fragrant.
  2. Combine sugar, honey, heavy cream, olive oil, and corn syrup in a small saucepan over medium low heat.
  3. Stir gently until the sugar dissolves and increase the heat to medium. Cook without stirring until slightly thickened.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in salt, vanilla and half of the rosemary.
  5. Add the toasted nuts and mix thoroughly until incorporated.
  6. Scrape the filling into a parbaked and cooled tart crust. Be sure to place tart pan on foil line baking sheet in case of spillage.
  7. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes, once the filling is bubbling and the surface is a deep golden brown.
  8. Let the tart cool completely.
  9. Remove the tart from the ring mold and top with rosemary and flaky salt before serving.
